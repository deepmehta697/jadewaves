What applications is Bentonite supplied for?

Bentonite is supplied for drilling fluids for oil, gas, and HDD, foundry and casting systems, and construction, sealing, and waterproofing work, with grade and sizing aligned to the end use.

What should be confirmed before ordering Bentonite?
