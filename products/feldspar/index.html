FAQ

Questions that usually come first.

Direct answers before pricing, sampling, and dispatch.

What applications is Feldspar supplied for?

Feldspar is supplied for ceramic and tile production, sanitaryware manufacturing, and glass production, with grade and sizing aligned to the end use.

What should be confirmed before ordering Feldspar?

Confirm potassium feldspar 10-12% K2O, sodium feldspar 8-10% Na2O, K2O% or Na2O%, and Fe2O3 limit so pricing, sampling, and dispatch can be aligned correctly.

How is Feldspar handled for export?

Supply formats include Jumbo Bags. Jade Waves aligns samples, export documents, and shipment planning from India to suit the destination and order size.
