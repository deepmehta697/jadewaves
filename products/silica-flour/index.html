FAQ

Questions buyers ask before the first order.

Direct answers before pricing, sampling, and dispatch.

What applications is Silica Flour supplied for?

Silica Flour is supplied for ceramics and tile bodies, paints and coatings, and construction fillers and specialty mixes, with grade and sizing aligned to the end use.

What should be confirmed before ordering Silica Flour?

Confirm mesh or micron grade, SiO2 purity, whiteness target, and packing and order volume so pricing, sampling, and dispatch can be aligned correctly.

How is Silica Flour handled for export?

Supply formats include 50 Kg/Jumbo Bags. Documents can include COA, COO, packing list, commercial invoice, bill of lading, and MSDS where relevant, aligned to the destination and order size.
