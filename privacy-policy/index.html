Privacy Policy

How enquiry information is handled.

If you contact Jade Waves through the website, email, phone, or WhatsApp, this page explains what information may be used and why.

What We Receive

Commercial information shared through enquiries.

01
Contact details

Name, company, email, phone number, and any details you share while making an enquiry.

02
Requirement details

Product, application, packing size, destination, volume, and technical points such as chemistry, sizing, or document needs.

03
Commercial follow-up

Messages shared through email, phone, or WhatsApp once the enquiry moves into sampling, quotation, or shipment planning.

Important Note

How the website form works today.

Website submissionEnquiry details are sent directly through the website form

UseQuote, sample, shipment planning, and document follow-up

How It Is Used

Only for enquiry, quotation, and supply handling.

01
Product fit and quotation

To understand the requirement, share the right product direction, and prepare pricing or sample discussion.

02
Packing and dispatch planning

To align bagging, destination, documents, and shipment basis to the enquiry.

03
Commercial follow-up

To answer questions, share updates, and manage repeat order communication where relevant.
