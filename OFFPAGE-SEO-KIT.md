## Anchor Text Rules

Use branded and partial-match anchors more often than exact-match anchors.

Recommended mix:

- `Jade Waves Enterprise`
- `Jade Waves Enterprise industrial minerals`
- `industrial minerals exporter from India`
- `quartz sand supplier from India`
- `feldspar supplier from India`
- `silica sand supplier from India`
- `bentonite supplier from India`
- `industrial salt supplier from India`

Do not overuse one exact phrase across every listing.

## Guardrails

Do not build links through bulk low-quality directory blasts.

Follow Google’s current spam guidance:

- `https://developers.google.com/search/docs/essentials/spam-policies#link-spam`

Use profiles that have:

- real company fields
- category alignment
- room for products and documents
- an actual public company page

Skip anything that exists only to sell “100 backlinks”.

## What Still Requires User-Owned Access

These actions still need the user because they involve accounts, payments, verification, or CAPTCHA:

- Google Business Profile verification
- LinkedIn Page creation
- YouTube channel creation
- Alibaba supplier profile setup
- go4WorldBusiness seller setup
- Yellow Pages UAE paid or form-based listing
- any marketplace that needs OTP, payment, or admin login
