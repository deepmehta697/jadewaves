# Google Search Console Checklist

## Important

You cannot add me directly as a Google Search Console user because I do not have a Google account or email address.

The workable setup is:
- you verify and use Search Console
- you share screenshots, exports, or copied data with me
- I use that data to guide SEO changes

## Setup steps

1. Open Google Search Console.
2. Add a `Domain` property for `jadewavesenterprise.com`.
3. Copy the DNS TXT record Google gives you.
4. Add that TXT record in your domain/DNS provider.
5. Wait for DNS to propagate, then click `Verify`.
6. Submit this sitemap:
   - `https://jadewavesenterprise.com/sitemap.xml`

## What to send me after setup

- Screenshot of the verified property
- Screenshot of `Performance > Search results`
- Screenshot of `Indexing > Pages`
- Screenshot of `Sitemaps`
- Exported top queries CSV if available
- Exported top pages CSV if available
